Shipping and Delivery

Free shipping on every single order

No minimums, no fine print, just a simple promise to get your new furniture home as soon as we can, for free. Other furniture companies charge outrageous special freight fees to put one couch on one truck, which is a costly, and not to mention unsustainable, way of doing things. But thanks to our modular design, we pack things in manageable boxes and ship them to you just like a normal package.

A quick word on delivery timelines

Weve always prided ourselves on some of the fastest delivery timelines in the business. Before the pandemic, we delivered orders within a week, and we intend to get back to that standard. Until then, we encourage you to check the shipping estimates, which can be found below or on the product page for any given item, right under the Add to Cart button. You can also take a look at our Ready to Ship section, which has everything thats in stock and will arrive within four weeks.

Please note: Due to increased demand and a reduction in fulfillment operations to maintain safe and healthy working conditions at our manufacturing and fulfillment centers, most of our seating is shipping on a slightly delayed schedule. In addition, some upholstery options are experiencing longer delays, as we await inbound shipments of materials from our suppliers.

Wondering how many boxes to expect? Generally, youll receive one more box than the number of seats you ordered, plus additional boxes for pieces like an ottoman or chaise. So if you ordered a standard 3-seat sofa with an ottoman, youll get 5 boxes. Each box weighs around 40 pounds and fits through just about any door.

Our simple and intuitive Range Collection ships directly from our warehouse to your door.

Each seat box weighs around 40 pounds and fits through just about any door. All other boxes weigh between 10 and 20 pounds each.

Delivery details for the Chorus Bed

Thanks to our clever design, the Chorus Bed ships in just a few boxes via UPS depending on the size you order.

  • Full bed: ships in 2 boxes, or 3 with headboard
  • Queen bed: ships in 2 boxes, or 3 with headboard
  • King bed: ships in 3 boxes, or 4 with headboard

Please note that shipping can vary based on the product and size. -

Delivery details for the Lyric Mattress

The Lyric Mattress arrives in a single box via UPS and will be wrapped in plastic and vacuum-sealed. Once unwrapped, it will take at least 3 hours to expand and up to 36 hours to reach the intended firmness.

Delivery details for the Prospect Dresser

Our Prospect Dresser comes ready to assemble in a few easy-to-move boxes depending on the size ordered.

  • Prospect Low Dresser (3 Drawers): ships in 3 boxes
  • Prospect Wide Dresser (6 Drawers): ships in 6 boxes
  • Prospect Low 3 Wide Dresser (9 Drawers): ships in 9 boxes
  • Prospect Tall Dresser (5 Drawers): ships in 3 boxes
  • Prospect Tall Wide Dresser (10 Drawers): ships in 6 boxes
  • Prospect Tall 3 Wide Dresser (15 Drawers): ships in 9 boxes

Delivery details for the Prospect Nightstand

Our Prospect Nightstand comes ready to assemble in 1 box.

Weve partnered with SIJO, an award-winning bedding brand, to offer an assortment of sheets, pillow inserts, duvet covers, duvet inserts, and mattress protectors. Sheets and duvets are available in standard Full, Queen, & King sizes.

The Vesper lounge chair arrives in a single box, and the ottoman, if you ordered one, will arrive in a separate box.

Large orders for trade & commercial accounts are handled differently from normal residential orders. They may take longer to ship, depending on the size of the order, the variety of products, and where in the country they're being delivered.
